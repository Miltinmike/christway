2017 Year Message

"Do it all for the glory of God"

Do it all for the glory of God Grace and peace from God our Father and from our Lord Jesus Christ, be unto us all in the year 2017 and beyond. Blessed be the God and Father of Our Lord Jesus Christ who has blessed us with all material spiritual blessings in Christ Jesus in the past years. He is our hope in the New year for sustainability and continuity of this grace. Our journey through 2017 should be made with a sincere determination to “do it all for the Glory of God”. (1 Cor. 10:31).

We should avoid the danger of self-deception because a hidden sin that is not tolerated but allowed to flourish leaves people to humiliation sonf disgrace. Remember your sin will find you out and make you lose respect, influence, reputation and love. Mores, sinful behaviour dishonours God and brings disgrace to the name of Jesus Christ, compromising your witnessing for Christ Jesus.

Sin commitment makes living joyless because built relationship between you as a Christian and Christ Jesus becomes unhealthy and makes our love for Christ Jesus grow cold. We should be trustworthy and dependable Christians who should be above reproach like Daniel who did it all for the glory of God.

1 Chronicles 29:17a says I know also, my God, that thou tries the heart and has pleasure in uprightness inconsistencies in any areas of our lives makes us self-contradictory and ineffective Christians. Sins are hazardous and roadblocks that threaten our spiritual journey, therefore we should resolve not to allow our behaviours to be used as gates through which the devil try to enter our lives. We should live our lives for Christ Jesus without backsliding.

Our watchword for 2017 Psalm 143 v 11 says “Quicken me, O Lord, for thy name’s sake, for thy righteousness sake bring my soul out of trouble”
Our Souls shall be consecrated unto our Lord for His glory in Jesus name.

Prophetess G.O. Fadeyibi JP
