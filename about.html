Our Believes

"Garments"

Sin commitment makes living joyless because built relationship between you as a Christian and Christ Jesus becomes unhealthy and makes our love for Christ Jesus grow cold. We should be trustworthy and dependable Christians who should be above reproach like Daniel who did it all for the glory of God.
